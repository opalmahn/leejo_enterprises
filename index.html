About LEEJO ENTERPRISES

For over two decades, LEEJO ENTERPRISES has been a cornerstone of quality craftsmanship and reliable service in Ruiru, Kimbo. Founded and led by John Kibe, a seasoned artisan with 20+ years of hands-on experience, we have built a reputation for excellence, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of our clients' unique needs.

We pride ourselves on our team of skilled and dedicated employees, each trained under the careful guidance of John Kibe himself. This ensures that every project, whether big or small, is executed with precision, durability, and the true spirit of Jua Kali innovation. We don't just build products; we forge lasting solutions.

From intricate metalwork to robust fabrication, our passion lies in transforming raw materials into functional and aesthetic pieces that stand the test of time. We believe in empowering local talent and delivering value that goes beyond the price tag.
